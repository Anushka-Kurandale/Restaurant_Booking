Our Story

Puneri Tadka Restaurant was founded in 2013 by Chefs Anushka,Sakshi,Snehal.Inspired by them travels and their love for Indian cuisine, they set out to bring the flavors of the world to a charming corner of the city. What started as a modest Puneri Tadka has now blossomed into a culinary destination, known for its innovative dishes and timeless classics.
